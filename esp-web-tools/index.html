Splendida LED Art

Firmware Updater

Update Your Device

Connect your Splendida LED Art piece to update to the latest firmware.

Instructions

  1. Plug in your Splendida LED Art piece
  2. Connect to USB port
  3. Click "Connect"
  4. Wait for the update to complete
  5. Your device will restart automatically
